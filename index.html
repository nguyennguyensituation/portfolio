About Me

I'm a Mascot Designer, Illustrator, Author, UX Writer, Product Manager,
Software Developer.

I chose the classic freelance mascot designer to software engineer career path, with a series of creative and tech jobs along the way. I've spent the last decade working in nearly every part of the tech ecosystem, from product strategy, design, and content creation; to building and supporting software.

The common thread among all these roles is that they gave me the opportunity to do the two things I love most: solve problems A Few of My Favorite Problems

1. I once had to perform emergency mascot surgery to ensure that a certain beer mascot would be ready in time for the Super Bowl.

2. Frustrated with how confusing and inefficient my new hire training was, I learned how to code and I built a learning app to replace the old training program. It was eventually adopted by multiple departments and used by hundreds of employees at my company.

3. Tasked with teaching a team of non-devs Git, I developed and led Git trainings based entirely around Post Malone's face tattoos. For months after, you could find little cutouts of Post Malone's face scattered in every corner of the office. and build beautiful things out of nothing. I doesn't matter if I'm solving problems for a Fortune 500 company, a teammate, or an end user in Indiana—I always bring creativity, technical expertise, and good humor to every new project.
